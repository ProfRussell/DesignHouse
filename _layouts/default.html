About Us

The Design House is a comprehensive online course design facility within the Learning Innovation Centre at Lambton College. It focuses on creating dynamic, learner centered content that encourages student engagement in a virtual environment. The Design House has many collaborative partnerships with other post-secondary institutions that produce fully online programs.
